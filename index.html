Gyro Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (any type works)
  • 6 tsbp. of tzatziki
  • 1/4 cup of lettuce
  • A couple of Spinach leaves
  • 2 oz. of cubed, cooked, meat (again, anything works here)
  • One shallot
  • One tomato

Steps

  1. Toast your bread until it's a bit brown. Make sure not to over toast your bread.
  2. Once your bread is toasted, put half of the tzatziki on both sides of the bread.
  3. Slice your lettuce, tomato, and shallot.
  4. Put your lettuce, tomato, and shallot on both sides of the bread.
  5. Put your meat on one peice of bread.
  6. Put the rest of your tzatziki on the meat.
  7. Close your sandwich.
  8. Enjoy!

Bomb Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Mayo
  • Guacamole
  • 2 Jalapenos, or crispy Jalapenos
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • One Tomato
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Toast your bread.
  2. Put your toast down and put a generous amount of mayo and guacamole on both peices.
  3. Cook/microwave your bacon, and put 2 on each the toast peices.
  4. Crack an egg in a separate container and mix it together. Then heat up a pan with some oil (I use olive oil) and cook your egg.
  5. Once the egg is done cooking, put it one of the peices of toast.
  6. Pull out a cutting board and slice your tomato. Then put it on a one of the peices of toast.
  7. Slice your jalapenos if you need to, then put them one of the peices of toast. If you have crispy jalapenos, just put them directly on.
  8. Close your sandwich.
  9. Enjoy!

Chicken Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 peices of bread
  • 2 oz. of cooked, cut chicken
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 tsbp. of grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put your cheese on both peices of bread.
  2. Put your chicken on one of the peices of bread.
  3. Close your sandwich.
  4. Heat up a pan.
  5. Once your pan is hot, put your sandwich on and cook/toast it while flipping it with a spatula every couple of minutes.
  6. Once the cheese is melting and your bread is slightly golden, take it off the pan.
  7. Eat and enjoy!
